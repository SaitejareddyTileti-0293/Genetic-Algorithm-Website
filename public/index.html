Genetic Algorithm

What is Genetic Algorithm?

Genetic Algorithm (GA) is a search-based optimization technique based on the principles of Genetics and Natural Selection. It is frequently used to find optimal or near-optimal solutions to difficult problems which otherwise would take a lifetime to solve.

Darwinian Natural Selection

Heredity

There must be a process in place by which children receive the properties of their parents

Variation

There must be a vareity of traits present in the population or a means with which to introduce variation such as Mutation

Selection

There must be a mechanism by which some members of population are chosen and their genes are passed down while others do not. This is typically called "Survival of the fittest"

Process involved

Initialize

Create a population of N elements,each with randomly generated DNA

Selection

Evaluate the fitness of each element of the population and build the mating pool based on their fitness

Reproduction

This is process is repeated until the population size is reached

  • Pick two parents probability according to their fitness
  • CrossOver :: Create a "child" by combining the DNA of these two parents
  • Mutation :: Mutate the child's DNA based on given probability
  • Add the new child to a new population

Repeat

Replace the old population with new population and repeat the process from Selection
